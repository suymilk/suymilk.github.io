I’m a designer & developer based in beautiful Aoteaora / New Zealand.

I have nearly a decade of experience in designing and coding online experiences, but my first love was journalism, and I am a storyteller first and foremost.

I love creating connection and capturing the magic of the moments in between. These days, I’m working on an illustrated record of the immigrant experience. If you’d like to share your story or collaborate, please say hello@suyeonson.com.

Over the course of a year, the elite Stuff Circuit team released a series of in-depth investigations, each designed to probe, analyse, and uncover an issue of vital importance to New Zealand.
